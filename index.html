Who We Are

Local. Reliable. Affordable.

Arizona Tires was founded in 2010 by a San Antonio local who saw his neighbors struggling to afford basic tire service. Too many families were driving on unsafe tires simply because quality service felt out of reach.

We started with a simple mission: give every driver in our community access to safe, reliable tires at prices that make sense. No pressure sales. No confusing upsells. Just honest, fast service from people who actually care.

Today, we've served over 5,000 customers across San Antonio and nearby communities. From single moms stretching a budget to small business owners keeping their fleets rolling — we're here for all of them.
